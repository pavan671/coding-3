Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management (GITAM), formerly GITAM University and GITAM College of Engineering, is a private institute, located in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. It was founded in 1980 by M. V. V. S. Murthi.

The university offers 109 programs at undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral levels in technology, pharmacy, science, management, international business, architecture and law. It is one of the few colleges in India which provide master's degree in every field.
